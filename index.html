Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a garage cleanout cost?

Our cleanouts start at $950 for standard jobs. Pricing depends on garage size, how packed it is, amount of hauling, type of debris, and labor intensity. Most full cleanout jobs start at $1,500. We provide a free, no-obligation quote before you commit to anything.

How fast can you schedule a cleanout?

Most jobs are scheduled within 24–72 hours. We also offer emergency/rush scheduling for situations that need immediate attention. Call us directly at 612-207-8736 if you need something fast.

Do you haul everything away or just clean?

Both! We offer full-service haul-out where we remove and dispose of everything, or we can do cleaning and organization if you've already dealt with the junk. Most of our clients want everything removed — furniture, appliances, junk, trash, debris. We handle it all.

What if my garage is extremely packed or overwhelming?

That's exactly who we work for. We specialize in packed, overwhelming garages — the jobs most people don't know where to start with. We've seen it all and handled it all. Don't be embarrassed — just reach out and we'll take it from there.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Pristine Garage Co is fully licensed and insured in Minnesota. You can have complete peace of mind that your property and belongings are protected throughout the entire process.

Do I need to be home during the cleanout?

You should be home at the start to walk us through what stays and what goes. After that initial walkthrough, many clients leave us to work. We'll check in if we have any questions and update you when the job is complete.

What cities do you serve in the Twin Cities area?

We serve the entire Twin Cities metro including Minneapolis, St. Paul, Edina, Eden Prairie, Bloomington, Burnsville, Plymouth, Minnetonka, Eagan, Lakeville, Apple Valley, Rosemount, Woodbury, Maple Grove, and surrounding communities. Not sure if you're in our area? Call us — chances are we can help.
